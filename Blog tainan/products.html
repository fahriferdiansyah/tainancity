Chimei Museum

The CHIMEI Museum is a comprehensive museum with a vast collection of Western art, musical instruments, weapons and natural history, also conceptualized as a white house in the United States. Chimei Museum is also a meeting place for parents as well as local youth and foreigners.

Tainan Park

Tainan Park is a public park in Tainan which is usually used as a gathering place for families, friends or relatives from one country and sporting place. here there is also a place for sports and games for small children whose parents want to spend some free time chatting with children after a week of busy work activities.

Anping Beach

Anping Beach is a famous beach located in the western part of Tainan City. Anping Beach is also a tourist destination for locals and foreigners alike. Here is also the most beautiful place to enjoy the sunset on the west side while accompanying the waves of waves that make you feel calm to start tomorrow.
