Coffin Bread

Coffin Bread is a typical snack from Tainan City, this Coffin Bread is similar to toast filled with vegetables such as peas, carrots, corn, and meat, chicken, and squid.

Beef Noodle Soup

Beef noodle soup, is a noodle soup made of stewed or red braised beef, beef broth, vegetables and Chinese noodles. It exists in various forms throughout East Asia and Southeast Asia.

Stinky Tofu

Stinky tofu is one of the traditional Chinese snacks. Stinky tofu is a kind of tofu that is fermented further so that it smells bad. The presentation is various, can be fried, steamed, boiled or roasted. What is unique, after being fried, boiled, steamed or roasted, this tofu doesn't smell bad anymore and can be a very tasty snack.
